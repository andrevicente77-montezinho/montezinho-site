The House

A space to slow down

Renovated in 2023, on the shore of the Maranhão reservoir — the interior comfort was updated, while the traditional Alentejo character was kept outside.

Ideal for 4 guests across 2 bedrooms. Inside, you'll find high ceilings, a workspace and a kitchen ready to use — outside, centuries-old olive trees and the quiet typical of inland Alentejo.

A place to return to what matters — the memories you already have, and the ones you'll build here.

During your stay, you have exclusive access to the whole property, except for the upper floor of the house.

Outdoors

Where the days grow longer

A 3-hectare olive grove, a sunlit pool, and just a five-minute walk away… the breathtaking waters of the Maranhão reservoir.

Pool

The old irrigation tank was converted into a bright pool, perfect for cooling off on hot Alentejo days.

Terrace and pergola

A wide west-facing terrace for long meals and conversations with family or friends.

Lake access

A five-minute walk through the olive grove to the Maranhão reservoir, for swimming in calm, quiet waters.

Bikes, canoe and SUP board

Available at no extra cost, to explore the lake at your own pace.

Yoga deck

A wooden deck right by the water, just steps from the lake's edge.

We loved our stay at Montezinho and couldn't recommend it more! It's such a beautiful, peaceful place right by the reservoir. We loved being able to simply grab a canoe or a paddleboard and head out onto the water — and having all the equipment ready to use was fantastic.
